Who We Are

A council built by photographers,
for photographers.

The International Photographers Council was founded with a single purpose: to create a body that takes photography seriously — one that recognises genuine talent, connects professionals across borders, and gives the craft the institutional weight it deserves.

Based in Qatar and operating internationally, the IPC runs recognition programmes, hands-on workshops, and curated exhibitions. We work with photographers at every stage — from emerging talent to established professionals seeking broader recognition.

Membership isn't just a title. It's a signal — to clients, to peers, and to the industry — that your work meets a standard worth acknowledging.

What We Do

Three pillars of the council.

◈

Recognition

An annual awards programme that identifies and celebrates work across portrait, automotive, landscape, documentary, and commercial photography. Judged by industry professionals.

◎

Education

Workshops, masterclasses, and critique sessions led by working photographers. From fundamentals to advanced post-production — practical skills that translate to real results.

⬡

Community

A network of verified photographers, shared resources, and collaborative opportunities. The people you want in your corner when a client brief needs a second opinion.
