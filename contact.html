FAQ

Quick answers.

How do I book a shoot?

Use the form above — it opens a formatted email with everything I need. Or DM on Instagram @egsk.raw. Both work, both get a fast reply.

Do you shoot rollers?

Yes. Rollers are available as an add-on. Tell me your preferred route, time of day, and the style you're after and I'll plan around it.

How fast is turnaround?

Typically 1–2 business weeks depending on scope. I'll always confirm the expected delivery timeline before we shoot.

Do you travel within Qatar?

Doha and surrounding areas are easy. For farther locations we'll factor travel into the plan and pricing.

What file formats are delivered?

Full-resolution JPEGs for print and archiving plus web-optimised versions sized for Instagram. RAW files available as an add-on.

Can I use the images commercially?

Usage rights are included for personal and social media. Commercial licensing for brands and dealerships is quoted separately — ask when you book.
